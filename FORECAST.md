# 2023-2024 SEASON FORECAST (The Neural Overhaul)

## Strategic Objective
Transition from structural MIDI generation to high-fidelity "Neural Texture Mapping" and automated peak-time production.

## Milestone 1: The AI Sound Design Layer (Q1-Q2)
- **Deep Suno/Udio Integration**: Fully automated browser-based rendering using headless CDP (Chrome DevTools Protocol).
- **Prompt Engineering Engine**: Automated GPT-4-based prompt generation based on hymn mood and target genre.
- **Local MusicGen Inference**: Integration of Meta's MusicGen for offline, high-speed sound design iteration.

## Milestone 2: Neural Mastering & Vocal Quality (Q2-Q3)
- **Mel-Roformer Integration**: High-fidelity vocal cleaning and enhancement.
- **Automated Mastering**: Use of Matchering or proprietary neural mastering chains to hit -7 LUFS festival standards.

## Milestone 3: Visual & Social Automation (Q3-Q4)
- **Generative Music Videos**: Automated FFmpeg-based video generation with audio-reactive visuals (Stable Video Diffusion).
- **Social Ingestion**: Automated upload to YouTube, TikTok, and SoundCloud with metadata optimization.

## Milestone 4: Architectural Scaling (Q4)
- **Multi-Genre Presets**: Expansion to Melodic Techno, Uplifting Trance, and Hardstyle.
- **Music21 Migration**: Deep harmonic analysis for better counterpoint preservation.
