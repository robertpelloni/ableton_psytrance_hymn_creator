# VISION

**Current Status: Version 1.0.0 - Phase 1 Complete.** The Autonomous Production Engine is fully operational, delivering high-quality Psytrance and House tracks derived from traditional hymns with zero manual intervention.

## Project Goal
Hymnmania Pipeline Engine is a fully automated, programmatic ecosystem that merges centuries-old religious hymn DNA with modern peak-time electronic music (Full-On Psytrance and Deep House).

## Core Foundational Concepts
1. **Symbolic Extraction**: High-fidelity parsing of classical MIDI files to extract harmonic anchors and melodic arrays.
2. **Algorithmic Transformation**: Deterministic conversion of solemn rhythms into high-velocity 145 BPM Psytrance and 124 BPM House frameworks.
3. **Neural Texture Mapping**: Utilizing generative AI (via extensible bridge endpoints) to morph sterile synthesizers into professional-grade sound design. Currently utilizing high-fidelity local synthesis (FluidSynth) and DSP enhancement (Pedalboard) as a baseline.
4. **Hybrid Orchestration**: Seamlessly blending algorithmic composition with AI-driven vocal separation and remixing.

## User-Satisfaction Design
- **Emotional Resonance**: Preserving the original melodic intervals and emotional depth of hymns.
- **Production Excellence**: Achieving festival-ready sound quality via neural mastering and AI sound design.
- **Hands-Free Creation**: A complete "Input MIDI -> Final Video" automated experience.
