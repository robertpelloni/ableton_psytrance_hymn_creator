# VISION

**Current Status: Version 1.5.0 - Phase 2 In Progress.** The Autonomous Production Engine has been upgraded to the "Psy-Mono" architecture, featuring advanced algorithmic sequencing and neural-ready stem rendering.

## Project Goal
Hymnmania Pipeline Engine is a fully automated, programmatic ecosystem that merges centuries-old religious hymn DNA with modern peak-time electronic music (Full-On Psytrance and Deep House).

## Core Foundational Concepts
1. **Symbolic Extraction**: High-fidelity parsing of classical MIDI files to extract harmonic anchors and melodic arrays.
2. **Algorithmic Transformation**: Deterministic conversion of solemn rhythms into high-velocity 145 BPM Psytrance (rolling bass, Euclidean leads) and 124 BPM House (four-on-the-floor, off-beat bass).
3. **Neural Texture Mapping**: Utilizing generative AI (via extensible bridge endpoints) to morph sterile synthesizers into professional-grade sound design.
4. **Vocal Remix Pipeline**: Programmatic isolation, time-stretching, and harmonic alignment of hip-hop vocals into electronic frameworks.

## User-Satisfaction Design
- **Emotional Resonance**: Preserving the original melodic intervals and emotional depth of hymns.
- **Production Excellence**: Achieving festival-ready sound quality via neural mastering and AI sound design.
- **Hands-Free Creation**: A complete "Input MIDI -> Final Video" automated experience.
